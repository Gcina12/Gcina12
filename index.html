WageBridge

An Earned Wage Access platform for South African SME employees, letting them access already-earned salary before payday. Built a full-stack system with a FastAPI backend, React dashboards for employees, employers, and admins, real Open Banking sandbox integration for bank account linking and consent, and a DebiCheck-style recovery simulation modeling how the platform reclaims advances once real salaries clear — without ever touching the employer's payroll batch.
